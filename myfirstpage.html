Bio

Xin Chen, an ensemble and solo trombonist based in UK(2024). Educated at Eastman School of Music in Rochester, NY, as an exchange postgraduate student for the year 2023-2024, and the Royal Birmingham Conservatoire in Birmingham, UK, as a postgraduate student from 2022 to 2024. Xin's foundation in music was laid at the Shanghai University School of Music, where he earned a Bachelor of Music from 2016 to 2020.

Under the tutelage of renowned educators like Mark Kellogg and Larry Zalkind at the Eastman School of Music, and direct influence from esteemed professionals such as Richard Watkin, Anthony Howe, Ed Jones, Amos Miller in the UK, Xin has developed a profound musical expertise and versatility. Participation in master classes with luminaries like Michael Buchanan, Weston Sprott, Joseph Alessi, Jesper Busk, Denson Paul Pollard, Mark Hampson further honed Xin's skills of alto trombone, tenor trombone and bass trombone.

Xin's ensemble experience is extensive, holding principal positions in the Shanghai Huju Opera House Orchestra from 2019-2021, also was the guest trombnist of Shanghai Philharmonic Orchestra, Shanghai Opera House Orchetra, Zhejiang Symphony Orchestra in China from 2019 to 2023, also served in Eastman Philharmonic Orchestra and the Eastman Wind Ensemble, Royal Birmingham Conservatoire Symphony Orchestra, The Amadeus Orchestra, West Yorkshire Symphony Orchestra and University of London Symphony Orchestra. Recognition for Xin’s talent includes the Turing Funding Scholarship from the Royal Birmingham Conservatoire in 2023 and a third prize in the First Conn-Selmer Trombone Online Competition in 2022.

Highlighted performances feature Xin's solo recital in China, played a versatile repertoire from standard to jazz pieces in 2023, and a performance at the Birmingham Lunar New Year Concert in 2023, where Xin was a soloist for the UK premiere of Gadameilin Thematic Fantasia. Xin’s commitment to musical excellence and a global perspective on performance continues to define a promising career in the world of music.
